Turning Surplus

Welcome to mealConnect - a platform on a mission to bridge the gap between surplus and scarcity. By connecting restaurants with extra food to local food banks and shelters, we aim to reduce food waste and fight hunger in our communities. Join us in making a difference, one meal at a time!

About Us

At MealConnect, we believe that no meal should go to waste while others go hungry. Our platform bridges the gap between restaurants with surplus food and local food banks and shelters in need. By facilitating quick, easy, and real-time food donations, we aim to reduce food waste and contribute to alleviating food insecurity in communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce food waste by connecting those who have surplus to those who are in need, fostering a community of support and sustainability.

Our Vision

To create a world where surplus food is redirected to those in need, ensuring that no one goes hungry, and every meal is used to its fullest potential.
